# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 6, 2026  
**Effective Date:** When app launches

---

## 1. Introduction

SoStonk is a professional strategy backtesting and chart analysis tool for iOS, macOS, and visionOS. Your privacy is important to us.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

**We collect NO personal information.**

All data is stored exclusively on your device:
- Trading strategies you create
- Backtest results and performance metrics
- Chart preferences and view settings
- Symbol watchlists and data
- Price action indicator configurations

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- ✅ **All data stored locally** on your device (SQLite database)
- ✅ **Optional iCloud sync** - only if you enable it in device settings
- ✅ **We never access, store, or transmit your data** to any server
- ✅ **No cloud storage** - we don't have any servers
- ✅ **End-to-end private** - your strategies are yours alone

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

SoStonk integrates with the following services **for data retrieval only**:

- **Apple StoreKit 2** - for processing in-app purchases (Playback, Price Action)
- **Binance API** - for cryptocurrency price data (public data only)
- **Yahoo Finance** - for stock and commodity data (public data only)

**Important:** We do NOT share any of your personal data with these services. We only fetch publicly available market data.

---

## 5. In-App Purchases

When you purchase features like Playback or Price Action Toolkit:

- Payment is processed by **Apple via StoreKit 2**
- Your transaction ID is stored **locally on your device only**
- Your purchase history is **never sent to any server we control**
- We do not have access to your Apple ID or payment information
- Refunds are handled exclusively by Apple

---

## 6. Analytics and Tracking

- ✅ **No analytics** - we don't track your usage
- ✅ **No crash reporting** - we don't log error data
- ✅ **No user tracking** - we don't follow your behavior
- ✅ **No cookies or identifiers** - we don't fingerprint users
- ✅ **No data sharing** - your data never leaves your device

---

## 7. Device Permissions

SoStonk may request the following permissions (depending on platform):

- **Chart data access** - to save and load strategies
- **Clipboard access** - to copy/paste symbols or strategies
- **App Group containers** - for optional sync features

You can revoke these permissions anytime in device settings.
